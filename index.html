Purpose of Blood Usage

🚑 Emergency & Accidents>
  • Severe accidents often cause heavy blood loss.
  • Trauma patients need immediate blood transfusion.
  • Blood helps stabilize patients in critical situations.
  • Emergency surgeries require quick blood availability.
  • Blood transfusion can be life-saving in emergencies.
🏥 Major Surgeries>
  • Blood is required in heart surgeries, organ transplants, and orthopedic procedures.
  • Blood transfusion is given to replace blood loss during operations.
  • Platelets and plasma are also important for surgery patients.
  • Blood availability is essential for safe and successful surgeries.
🎗️ Cancer Treatment>
  • Cancer treatments like chemotherapy can reduce blood cell counts.
  • Patients often need red blood cells and platelet transfusions.
  • Blood transfusions help prevent infections and weakness.
  • Platelets are important to control bleeding during treatment.
  • Blood support improves recovery and overall patient care.
👶 Childbirth & Anemia>
  • Blood may be needed during childbirth due to heavy bleeding.
  • Women with severe anemia often require blood transfusions.
  • Blood helps maintain the mother’s strength and stability.
  • Timely transfusion ensures the safety of both mother and baby.
  • Blood support is important for recovery after delivery.
🧬 Blood Disorders>
  • Blood disorders like thalassemia and hemophilia require regular transfusions.
  • Patients often need red blood cells, platelets, or plasma.
  • Blood transfusions help manage symptoms and prevent complications.
  • Regular blood support improves quality of life for patients.
  • Long-term treatment of many blood disorders depends on blood availability.
